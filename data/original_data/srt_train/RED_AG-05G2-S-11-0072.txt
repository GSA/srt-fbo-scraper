1.0 GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS A. Scope of Contract The purchase order to be awarded as a result of this solicitation is for the supply of (2) linuxbased servers to be housed in and used in conjunction with the U.S. Forest Service's Seattle High Performance Computing Center located at the Pacific Wildland Fire Sciences Laboratory in Seattle, WA. The linux servers will function as an extended unit with existing linux servers. As such, there are specific technical requirements needed to ensure that they are compatible with the existing computers. 1) Technical requirements (basic) all machines of this type must meet: a) Rack-mountable, no larger than 3 U in size; b) Dual Giga-bit Ethernet ports. 2) Technical requirements (additional): a) 4 CPU chips; b) Each CPU chip shall be 8-core MINIMUM Intel Xeon processor; c) MINIMUM 1 GB of total onboard memory PER CPU CORE (4x8 = 32GB MINIMUM); d) Front-side bus speed of 1 GB +; e) MINIMUM 6 hot-swappable drive bays that can mount 3.5" SATA drives; f) RAID controller that can handle the full number of drives; g) At least one 2 TB drive included (Note: non-filled drive bays are acceptable as the Government has additional existing disks to fill in the empty bays); h) MINIMUM 3-year warranty. NOTE-1: (Items e) and g) above) that not all of the drive bays are filled. This condition reflects the Government's need to install Government property additional disks and to allow for future expansion of the systems.
