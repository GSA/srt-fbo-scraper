SECTION C

Statement of Work Access to a commercial business database that features general search, monitoring, and unique screening capabilities for domestic and international-based business entities. Qty: Not to exceed 10 DSS users. The database must contain the following features: 1. Identifiers:

HS0021-14-T-0116 Page 4 of 21

Database must assign unique identifiers such as an alpha-numeric code to allow for precise matching between entity name and DSS proprietary data sets. 2. Batch Import Capability: Database must be capable of uploading unlimited batch listings of DSS proprietary information, specifically by entity name and ticker symbol. 3. Monitoring/Alert Service: Database must include the ability to monitor and alert the user of business entity material change conditions against DSS batch listing of proprietary, private business structures such as all Securities Exchange Commission (SEC) filings to include but not limited to (8K, 13F, 13G), announced/completed transactions, mergers/acquisitions, bankruptcies, company forecasts/ratings, dividend and stock splits, ownership changes, public offerings, officer changes, deal announcements, corporate communications, and upcoming events. The service must offer delivery options ranging from customer personal email notification to batch listings. Results must be exportable to Microsoft Word and Excel. Platform must include the capability to internally screen, filter, and group private investment fund structures such as limited liability corporations, limited partnerships, hedge funds, sovereign wealth funds and other private entities, by primary geographic location. Platform must also have subset screening capability to group the data by investment categories such as direct investment, equity stake, ownership percentage, limited partnership interests, capital commitment level, and corporate structure.

4. Analytical Platform Tool/Screening Mechanism: Platform also must possess the capability to export previously developed screens and watch lists to Microsoft Word and Excel. Identify current and historical common stock positions by date/month/year. Stock positions must hyperlink directly to their respective SEC files. Data must be exportable to Microsoft Excel and Word. Screen private institutions for offshore capital contributions, by region/location. Identify private and public investors with ownership summary broken down by type and detailed information about stock holders. Stock holder information must be displayable alphabetically, by common stock equivalent held, percent of common stock etc. Provide customizable merger and acquisition charts containing historical timelines for M&A data. Display timelines for prior, current, and pending global investments for business entities of all types (corporations, limited liability corporations, partnerships, sole proprietorships, etc). Must be exportable to Microsoft excel and word.

5. Financial Statement Analysis:

HS0021-14-T-0116 Page 5 of 21

Search results must contain business financial data to include income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, financial multiples, capital structure, common ratios, industry specific information, and market segment details. Financial ratios must be precalculated. Financial information obtained by the service provider through SEC filings must be hyperlinked.

6. Management Personnel: Provide professional profiles and biographical information for chief executives, board directors/members, and other key professionals. Profiles must include title, office, job functions, board memberships) and business (public and private) affiliation information (both current and prior). Results must be exportable to Microsoft excel and word. Provide compensation information and options/award details for chief executives, board directors/members, and other key professionals.

7. Customer Support: Must provide customer service to assist users in tailoring and developing means of utilizing screening platform, and map DSS proprietary data using unique identifiers with datasets contained in commercial database. Period of performance: Base: 26 September 2014 ­ 25 September 2015 Option 1: 26 September 2015 ­ 25 March 2016 .
