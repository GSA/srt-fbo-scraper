1. BACKGROUND:
Tricare Management Activity (TMA) has identified a requirement to remove the existing mailroom and replace with a fully operational mailing system. The purpose of the mailing system is to mail letters and packages via USPS, track all inbound packages received and express ship all letters and packages via FedEx, and will include warranty, and postage meter rental. The current mailing and tracking system is 8 years old in place will need to be removed and discarded.

2. SCOPE:

This requirement is to acquire mailing equipment, establish warranty support, and rent a postage meter to bring the mailing system current to USPS standards in support of TMA. All equipment and services must meet the approval of the USPS. The Contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, etc. to provide a complete turn-key, fully functional mail room system and postage meter rental service in accordance with the specifications defined below.
