Scheduled Preventive Maintenance visits



Contractor will be responsible to provide technicians that will perform preventive maintenance, applicable firmware upgrades, and a complete system operation check twice a year during warranty period. This includes all labor associated with changing projection lamps, cleaning filters, etc. Firmware/software upgrades are limited to manufacturer-provided corrections and operational upgrades. Software upgrades or control system changes to provide additional functionality or enhanced operation are not included. System operation includes a complete functionality test, as well as a verification of the image and sound quality of all sources (BD, GFE-PC, etc.) and displays (projectors, LCD/LED monitors, etc.) Often, they will answer end-user system operation questions or provide informal basic end-user/operator training. A copy of the system checkout results may be requested by the client, identifying needed action items in order to prevent future system issues.



Unlimited Unscheduled Service visits



Customer Support Agreement must include unlimited unscheduled service visits. All technicians are to arrive within 8 business hours after determination on-site service is needed. Once on-site, they will perform necessary system diagnostics to isolate any issues within the system. Issues related to equipment configuration, set-up, or connectivity, are typically corrected immediately. System issues related to equipment failures must be escalated to other internal support teams to determine the most expeditious method required to restore system operation. This includes system "work arounds,” advanced equipment replacement, and equipment repair. 



Equipment Repair and Replacement Parts



Contractor will provide equipment requiring repair to be “triaged” in the local office to determine the quickest method of repair required to return the equipment to service and restore normal system operation. The costs associated with replacement parts, shipping, factory labor, and in-house labor are covered by the Customer Service Agreement.
