The United States Department of Labor’s Employee Benefits Security Administration (EBSA), Office of Technology and Information Services (OTIS) is seeking a contractor to provide the following:

Upgrades and maintenance to the Wide Area Network to facilitate connectivity of the field office Local Area Network’s to databases resident in the National Office.

Upgrades and maintenance of communications provided between all offices for electronic mail, sharing of database files, and access to data.

Other office automation software being upgraded to newer versions

Assurance of a corporate and mission related infrastructure that promotes efficiency and interoperability.

An indefinite quantity/indefinite delivery contract is anticipated, on a fixed unit price bases. This is a 100% set aside for small businesses.  The NAICS code for this requirement is 541519, Other Computer Related Services, with a size standard of $25,000,000.00.  The period of performance of the proposed contract is anticipated to encompass one base year plus four additional option years.  



The solicitation will be available on or after April 21, 2010 and will be distributed solely through the General Services Administration’s Federal Business Opportunities web site, .  Hard copies of the solicitation document will not be available.  The site provides downloading instructions.  All future information about this acquisition, including solicitation amendments, will also be distributed solely through this site.  Interested parties are responsible for monitoring this site to ensure that they have the most up-to-date information about this acquisition.  The estimated closing date for this solicitation will be on or about May 21, 2010.  All responsible sources may submit a proposal which will be considered by this agency.  No collect calls will be accepted.  No telephone requests for the solicitation will be accepted.
