                                                                   ATTACHMENT 1
                                                  “Brand Name or Equal” Salient Characteristics

*ALL PRODUCTS MUST MEET MUST BE SECTION 508 COMPLIANT.   ALL PRODUCTS MUST BE PURCHASED FROM “Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM)
Authorized/Certified Vendor’s Only”.

Salient Characteristics:  Software development to develop applications to be used on handheld computer and barcode scanners.  The tool must be
capable of developing applications for handheld computers/scanners that support the following capabilities.

    • Two way data push from the HMS application to the handheld device and from the handheld device to the HMS application;

    • Can route transactions to the proper users’ mobile device;
    • Can capture a signature electronically;
    • Can manage handheld devices remotely;
    • Provide advance barcode scanning support for automatic focus assignment after scanning a barcode.

    • Supports both wireless and docked data synchronization;

    • Capable of docked data synchronization with the server without a desktop ODBC connection;

    • Can deploy new applications to handheld devices based on user roles;

    • Provides full remote control of mobile devices;

    • Capable of preventing users from loading unauthorized applications on the mobile devices;

    • Supports the capture of digital photographs by the handheld computer/scanner

    • Supports the rapid development and deployment of applications without the use of custom code;

    • Capable of running on multiple devices (e.g. laptops and handhelds) without having to alter the application for each different device.

   Training: Training shall be provided to ensure that NARA achieves a high level of functional knowledge thereby ensuring their ability to build
   applications for handheld devices using the software tool with minimal support from the vendor.


   Maintenance Agreement:  The initial agreement will be for a one year period to begin following acceptance of installation and training.
   During this period the vendor is expected to provide the following services:
      • Technical phone support Monday – Friday during normal business hours;
      • Web Support tools including software downloads and knowledge base;
      • Maintenance and software updates;
      • Process for escalating technical issues.
