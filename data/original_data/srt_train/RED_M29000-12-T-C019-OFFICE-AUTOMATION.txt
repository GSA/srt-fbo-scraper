SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS
General.  This is a solicitation for a quote in support of Cobra Gold 2012.  The contractor shall provide all supervision and labor as necessary
to furnish the supplies as requested by the contracting officer.  Submit your proposal/Quote for review for a possible Blanket Purchase
Agreement  (BPA) for the items listed in this solicitation. This is only a request for proposal/Quote and does not constitute a contract.
PROCUREMENT APPROACH. It is the Government intent to issue one (1) firm-fixed-price award, utilizing FAR Parts 12 and 13 procedures.

BASIS FOR AWARD. The basis for award is Lowest Priced Technically acceptable.  To be technically acceptable, offeror shall have the capability
to deliver the quantity required and meet the delivery schedule as detailed in the below chart.

SUBMITTAL INFORMATION.  All quotes shall be received by the Government NLT 12:00 PM local time, Thailand, December 10, 2011. All quotes shall be
submitted via email to Maj Chris Rabassi at christopher.rabassi@usmc.mil and chris.rabassi@us.army.mil.
Quotes shall provide inclusive pricing for services and supplies to include maintenance and delivery fees.
   1. All Quoted pricing shall be good for 90 Days.
   2. Quotes shall include cancellation fees, if any.

The Apparent Successful offeror shall have satisfactory past performance. The Government intends to make one single award. The Government
reserves the right to make an award based on initial quotes received, without discussions.
Delivery Requirements.  All services rendered under the terms of this agreement must be accompanied by a delivery ticket.  All delivery tickets
shall include the following minimum information:
     a. Name of contractor
     b. BPA call number
     c. Date of call/request
     d. Order/call number
     e. Itemized list of supplies/services provided
     f. Quantity, price, extended price
     g. Place of delivery/performance
     h. Date of delivery or inclusive dates of service

These delivery tickets shall be authenticated by having the receiver sign and date the delivery ticket verifying that all services were rendered
or supplies were received in accordance with the agreement/call.

QUANTITIES
The Government reserves the right to change the awarded Contracts quantity by 20%, at any given time.  Changes to the Contract will be issued
in writing.

Invoice instructions
A summary invoice shall be submitted at least bi-weekly or upon expiration of this agreement, whichever occurs first, for all deliveries made
during a billing period for which payment has not been received.  These invoices must be supported by copies of delivery tickets.
Invoice date, name of contractor, agreement number, order/call number, period of service, unit price, quantity, total amount due, name and phone
number of person to contact in case of defective invoice, EFT data, and any other information required by agreement provisions.
Any questions that arise during the period of the agreement, to include but not limited to information regarding delivery, payment, or changes
shall be directed to the Contracting Office listed on page 1 of this agreement or a designated representative (any designated representative
will be appointed in writing by the contracting officer).
Payment Information
All payments under the agreement of this contract shall be administered by the JUSMAG.  Final payment will be made by: Electronic Funds Transfer
or Cash (local or U.S. currency).
 NO ADVANCE PAYMENTS ARE AUTHORIZED.  Payments will be made in cash upon or bank check made payable to the contractor.
Any questions that arise during the period of solicitation to include but not limited to information regarding delivery, payment, or changes
shall be directed to the Contracting Office listed on page 1 of this agreement or a designated representative (any designated representative
will be appointed in writing by the contracting officer).
