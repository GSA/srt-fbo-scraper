Statement of Work Television and Radio Monitoring

I. Objectives The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) wishes to engage a firm to monitor television and radio for mentions of the SEC and our issues in the top 50 media markets and on cable. The output would be access to an online tracking resource that collects all real-time mentions of SEC keywords, stores them online and sends email alerts when key terms that we follow are mentioned and provides us with instantaneous video and transcripts. II. Scope and Requirements This is a solicitation for the following services: 1. A Web-based service that will monitor national cable television and the top 50 media markets for radio and television; 2. A system that would enable the SEC to develop a set of keywords that will help capture information that is vital to our mission; 3. A service that would capture and store information, alerting SEC when keywords are mentioned; and 4. A service that will deliver the information to the SEC via the Web site in transcripts, video and/or audio. Office of Public Affairs (OPA) representatives would work with the vendor to establish keywords and desired channels and markets that at of importance to the SEC. III. Period of Performance The period of performance for the contract shall be a one-year base period plus four (4) one-year option periods. The anticipated award date is December 17, 2010.
