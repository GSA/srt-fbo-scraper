MAXIMO SUPPORT SERVICES PERFORMANCE WORK STATEMENT

1.0. Objective/Scope: The contractor shall provide non-personal technical support services in continuing maintenance and development of MAXIMO for Civil Engineer Division. Provided technical services will include Operational Support; Configuration Management Support; and Management Support. 1.0.1. Background: The 733d Civil Engineer Division (CED) utilizes the MAXIMO Asset Management System. This system has been in operation since 2003, and contains the CED minor maintenance workload database for tracking all service order work. This system is critical for mission accomplishment and must be maintained in fully operational status, thus requiring support services to maintain and update the MAXIMO software. 1.1. Description of Service. 1.1.1. Operational Support. The contractor shall provide operational support for systems components and make certain that MAXIMO is operating to support system users. Along with Help Desk Support, the contractor shall provide minor improvements/changes to system functionality to meet day-to-day requirements, such as application changes and report development. The contractor support shall also include training (either one-on-one or classroom), as required by CED, not to exceed one (1) day onsite per quarter to be coordinated between the contractor and the Contracting Officer's Representative (COR). 1.1.1.1. Configuration Management Support. The contractor shall provide configuration management support by defining requirements, planning, configuration, testing, deployment and documentation of system changes. The contractor shall maintain a replicated environment of the CED MAXIMO system and software configuration to serve as a diagnostic and testing environment. The contractor shall transfer of database data for usage on replicated system will be provided utilizing a file transfer protocol (FTP) site maintained by the contractor. The contractor shall properly document system changes, including recovery procedures and scripts. 1.1.1.2. Administrator Support. The contractor shall provide administrator support, for network communications, including technical support necessary to address isolated component issues that relate and affect overall system operations, Oracle Enterprise, MAXIMO Enterprise, Total Resource Management Rules Manager and related software. The contractor shall provide diagnostic support for performance or functional aberrations; installation of software maintenance and functional in version upgrades; and support for archiving, backup and recovery functions. Contractor shall provide onsite assistance quarterly for installation of JAVA required updates to maintain system integrity and meet applicable security requirements.

The contractor shall provide User Help Desk telephonically and on-site support, as needed, for the CED service order/help desk and MAXIMO users. The contractor shall provide support to help desk personnel who may have problems providing MAXIMO related support to the local Fort Eustis MAXIMO user community and to troubleshoot identified problems. The contractor shall provide development support which shall include creation and maintenance of reports and application creation and modification. Report development shall not exceed four (4) new report requirements during the contract year. Report modifications shall not exceed one (1) report modification per month. Application modifications shall be accomplished to best incorporate business rules utilizing Rules Manager when applicable and to reduce time and minimize system software alterations. 1.1.2. 2.4. Surveillance Methods. 2.4.1. 100 % Inspection. This method requires the COR to inspect the service each time it occurs. The COR will use the Quality Assurance Surveillance Checklist (QASC) during inspection. Receiving two or more unsatisfactory surveillances within a 12 month period may result in unsatisfactory past performance documentation. Continued receipt of unsatisfactory 100% surveillances during the contract period shall constitute a negative trend and the Contracting Officer may take any appropriate action in accordance with the FAR 52.212-4 (a), Contract Terms and Conditions - Commercial Items, Inspection/Acceptance. 2.4.2. Ombudsman (AUG 2005)

(a) An ombudsman has been appointed to hear and facilitate the resolution of concerns from offerors, potential offerors, and others for this acquisition.
